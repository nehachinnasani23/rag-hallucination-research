# Employee Benefits and Work Policy

[S1] Employees receive 12 paid vacation days per year.

[S2] Remote work is allowed up to two days per week with manager approval.

[S3] Health insurance begins on the first day of the month after 30 days of employment.

[S4] Employees may request reimbursement for approved work equipment up to $500 with receipts.

[S5] Job-related training courses may be reimbursed if the employee receives manager approval before enrollment.

[S6] Employees must submit expense reports within 30 days of purchase.
